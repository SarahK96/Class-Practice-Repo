No listed author, copyright Twitter, Inc.
http://www.nltk.org/nltk_data/
94.5 MB of twitter posts, JSON format, tagged for positive/negative mood
Does not contain a license document, but README file states that use is subject to Twitter Developer Agreement

Santa Barbara Corpus of Spoken American English
Authors: Du Bois, John W., Wallace L. Chafe, Charles Meyer, Sandra A. Thompson, Robert Englebretson, and Nii Martey
http://www.linguistics.ucsb.edu/research/santa-barbara-corpus
The corpus consists of audio files in .WAV and .MP3 formats, and corresponding transcripts in .trn and .cha (IRC Chat) formats
The SBCSAE is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-No Derivative Works 3.0 United States License.
The audio files contain recordings of many different types of natural conversation.
The transcriptions contain timestamps to match the audio files.
